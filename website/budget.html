Fuel Station

The price to purchase RoboPump for use in your gas station is £506 per robot. For installation via our team please contact robopump123@gmail.com where we will arrange a date and inform you of the installation additional charge.

The price of our robot is solely based of the price it costs us as manufacturers to produce the system which is detailed in the table above.

Customer

Our system is completey free for the end users to install on their mobile devices and use. RoboPump doesn't take any money from the customers, the only cost involved is the price of the fuel which is the charge from the gas station.
